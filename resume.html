Artist Statement

Leo Yan is an artist based in the Bay Area that works primarily in the digital 3D field to make environments, bringing his personal and unique ideas to life. In his work, he creates environments and spaces that evoke similar feelings that he experienced when playing video games as a kid. Using Blender as his primary tool, besides making something just visually appealing, he strives to create pieces that carry a personal significance for him. The majority of his pieces reflect memories, emotions, and experiences he had encountered throughout his life and artistic journey.
