Artist Statement

Artists: Andre Zambrana, Jonathan Torres, Leo Yan

This project was done in the CADRE club during the Spring semester of 2024 with my group. For this project, my group picked the design based on the picture of Tobey Maguire in a jumping pose since we thought it was unique but also funny at the same time. After picking our design, we printed it out as ordered layers to give it depth and laser cutted them. But working on the lightbox required the use of soldering LEDs to give the piece lighting behind it and the construction of a thin wooden box to hide the wires inside. It turned out to be really nice and it was my first time doing a group project in this club.
