Artist Statement

Artists: Ky Han Ly, Leo Yan, Bryan Ko, Daria Orlova, Hannah Yee, Hao Xu, Jennifer Low, Khoa Nguyen, Maleah Armenta, Minh Le, Rhyme Balabat

Each flower, like animals and humans, carries its unique beauty and meaning. The project invites artists to create their unique flowers, a reflection of their style and identity. The origin, bloom time, and lifespan of these flowers are not important. What matters is the powerful message they convey: that everyone possesses their natural charm. This visual celebration is not complete without the audience, who are invited to admire the beauty of the flowers and the diverse stories and personalities they represent.
