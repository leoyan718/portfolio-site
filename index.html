Leo Yan

Digital Media Artist

Emerging from the Bay Area, Leo Yan is a Digital Artist who graduated from San Jose State University in 2025 with a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Digital Media Art. Throughout his years studying, he has learned to work in many different mediums including 3D modeling, creative coding, generative art, interactive art, video editing, animation and projection mapping. His experience through these varieties of different mediums he practiced influences how he approaches to work on his projects. He works primarily in Blender with a focus on creating original expressive characters and environments inspired by his personal experiences in life.
