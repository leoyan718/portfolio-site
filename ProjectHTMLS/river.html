River Flowed in You (2024)


3D project


×

Artist Statement



In this project, I created a render of an old piano flowing down a river in Blender. This is a personal project for me as the title of the piece is based on the piano song River Flows With You by Yiruma. It was the first song I learned when I got a piano in 2020 during quarantine and the only song I knew how to play remarkably well. But over the years, ever since I learned the piano, it's mainly been on and off. I would sometimes play a few times a week, to a few times a month, then to a few times a year so the piano would just sit and collect dust. So this is a personal project that I did to express how playing the piano for me has been getting less and less and I wanted to symbolize how it’s basically like flowing away down a stream. I also added a little detail about the 4 rocks along the stream to symbolize mainly how it’s been 4 years as well.
