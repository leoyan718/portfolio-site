Artist Statement

Between Past and Present, is a personal project that dives deep into my life and reflects themes including my memories and change I experienced as I grew up. With the use of 3D modeling as my medium, I want to show how my bedroom, a space where I had countless memories in, can shift and transform as I did. This project reflects my growth from a joyful and innocent childhood to a rather complex and the reality of adulthood which included hardships. A few of the hardships included being more independent, having responsibilities, and facing struggles. As a child, the world felt more simple and carefree with less responsibilities and struggles that I had to worry about. But as I grew older, I realized that adulthood also comes with many expectations. As I model each version of the bedroom, I utilize various visual changes such as how bright colors start to fade into more muted ones, loved objects being added/ replaced, and some even quietly fading away without a trace. Each transformation reflects the changes I went through when growing up such as the start of new interests or hobbies I encountered while the old ones fade slowly and become forgotten little by little. Through this project, I hope for the audience to also reflect on their own spaces and the ways they have evolved through time. Our living spaces contain pieces of memories that show who we are and how we have grown. By displaying these changes through my personal life, I hope to express a sense of nostalgia and encourage others to reflect on how their personal spaces hold memories that have shaped their growth and identity.
